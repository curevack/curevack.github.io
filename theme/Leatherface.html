Leatherface

Leatherface's Past

Jedidiah Sawyer has no direct history or explanation of childhood, but the Sawyer family and Jed hold a dark secret that everyone in their town knows quite well. The Sawyers are a tight knit family that use Jed or otherwise known as Leatherface (nickname given because he wears real faces) as the brawn of their family. Shown to be primarily acting in self-defense, Jed cares for his family even though he has been abused and quite evidently neglected with his lack of knowledge on even the most basic aspects of humanity. He and his family are a group of cannibals who fool and capture visitors through a ploy of Jeds brother pretending to be a hitchhiker. Cannibalizing, and fashioning the parts into furniture is just simply the way this family bonds.

Leatherface's Capabilities

Although Jed has not been shown to have any supernatural capabilities, he does possess on some sense inhuman strength. Countless times on screen he has been shown effortlessly swinging and running for long durations with a big chainsaw, and also shows remarkable skill with a hammer. Aside from strength, this Sawyer is running long and fast even when lugging a big chainsaw in his hands. Best stay off his property!
